To the Bottom! is Mario meets an infinite faller, where you play as an adventurer trying to reach the bottom of a seemingly infinite hole, filled with platforms and enemies to make it harder than you might think! Can you find the bottom? or will you wake up at the edge of the hole, safe and sound, just to start all over again?
